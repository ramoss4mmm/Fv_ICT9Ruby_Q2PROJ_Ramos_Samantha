Biko

Sources

Ingredients:

2 cups glutinous rice (sticky rice or malagkit)

1 1/2 cups water

2 cups brown sugar

4 cups coconut milk

1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

1.Combine the sticky rice and water in a rice cooker and cook until the rice is ready (combine lesser amount of water than the usual so that the rice will not be fully cooked).

2.Combine the coconut milk with brown sugar and salt in a separate pot and cook in low heat until the texture becomes thick, then stir constantly.

3.Add the cooked rice in the coconut milk and sugar mixture then mix well, then ontinue cooking until all the liquid evaporates (do not overcook).

4.Scoop the cooked biko and place it in a serving plate then flatten the surface.
