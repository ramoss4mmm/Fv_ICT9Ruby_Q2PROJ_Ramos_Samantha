Sisig

Sources

Ingredients:

1 pound pig ears

1 pound pig snout

1 pound pork belly

2 pieces onions (minced)

3 pieces bay leaves

2 teaspoons salt

4 thumbs ginger (crushed)

1 quarts water

Instructions:

1.Combine pig ears, snout, pork belly, ginger, bay leaves, 2 teaspoons salt, and 1 quart of water in a cooking pot, boil for 1 hour, then remove from the pot and drain the water.

2.Heat-up your grill, start grilling the pig parts for around 5 minutes per side, then remove from the grill afterwards and let it cool down.

3.Make the dressing by combining all the dressing ingredients.then mix well.

4.Chop the grilled pig parts into small pieces. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped pork with onion and the dressing mixture, then toss until all the ingredients are well blended.
