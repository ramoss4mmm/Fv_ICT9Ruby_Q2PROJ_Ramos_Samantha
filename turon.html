Turon

Sources

Ingredients:

6 pcs bananas saba or plantains, cut in half (lengthwise)

1 cup jackfruit (ripe and sliced)

1 1/2 cup sugar

12 pcs lumpia wrapper

2 cups cooking oil

Instructions:

1.Roll the banana on a sugar plate and make sure that the banana is coated with enough sugar.

2.Place the banana with the sugar coating on the lumpia wrapper, then add a slice of ripe jackfruit on top.

3.Fold and lock the spring roll wrapper by using water to seal the edge.

4.In a pan, heat the oil and put-in some sugar, then wait until the brown sugar floats.

5.Put the wrapped banana and fry, wait until the wrapper turns golden brown and the extra sugar sticks on wrapper.
