Pancit

Sources

Ingredients:

1 (12 ounce) package dried rice noodles

1 teaspoon vegetable oil

1 onion (diced)

3 cloves garlic (minced)

2 cups cooked chicken breast meat (diced)

1 small head cabbage (thinly sliced)

4 carrot (thinly sliced)

Â¼ cup soy sauce

2 lemons (cut into wedges)

Instructions:

1.Place rice noodles in a large bowl, then cover with warm water and let soften for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2.Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and garlic, then cook and stir until onion is tender, about 3 to 5 minutes. After Stir in chicken, cabbage, carrots, and soy sauce, then cook until cabbage begins to soften.

3.Toss in noodles and cook, stirring constantly, until heated through.

4.Transfer pancit to a serving dish and garnish with lemon wedges.
