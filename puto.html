Puto

Sources

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 cup granulated white sugar

3/4 cup fresh milk

1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

3/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)

2 tbsp butter (melted)

1 egg

5 cups water (for steaming)

Instructions:

1.Pour the water into a steamer or cooking pot, then let boil.

2.Sift flour, sugar, and baking powder, combine in a bowl, then mix well.

3.Gradually add the milk and egg while stirring, then Continue to stir until the texture of the mixture becomes smooth.

4.Stir-in the vanilla extract, butter, and half of the cheese.

5.Pour the mixture into small to medium sized molds. Arrange in a steamer, then cover and steam for 25 minutes. Top each puto with the remaining cheese. Steam for 5 minutes more.

6.Take the steamed cheese puto out of the steamer. Let it cool down.

7.Gently remove the puto from the mold with the help of a spatula.
