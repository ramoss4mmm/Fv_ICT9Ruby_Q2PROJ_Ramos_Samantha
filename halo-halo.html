Halo-Halo

Sources

Ingredients:

1/4 cup small tapioca pearls (optional)

3 saba bananas (2 regular bananas)

1 cup water, plus more for the pearls

3/4 cup packed light brown sugar

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

1.Cook the tapioca (optional)

2.Slice and peel the bananas crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick rounds.

3.Make the syrup. Place 1 cup water, light brown sugar, and kosher salt in a large saucepan. Stir and bring to bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to maintain a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.

4.Add the bananas and reduce the heat to maintain a low simmer and cook until tender and translucent, about 5 minutes.

5.Add the tapioca. Add 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract and tapioca if using, and stir to combine, breaking up any clumps. Transfer to a medium bowl and let cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate until cold, about 2 hours.

Cut the jackfruit. Remove the jackfruit from a 20-ounce can, leaving the syrup behind. Coarsely chop the jackfruit into bite-sized pieces. Return the jackfruit back to the can.

Make the milk mixture. Place 2/3 cup coconut milk, 3 tablespoons evaporated milk, and 3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk in a small bowl or liquid measuring cup and stir to combine.
